22641-PM1 WINDER PULPER CONSISTENCY CONTROLPM1 winder pulper consistency control is not controlling consistency as it should which is causing chronic plugged line to outside broke chest. Investigation findings:- Pm1 winder has to dilution water valves (loops 1766A and 1766B). These valves are controlled from DCS with 4-20MA output to each valves pneumatic I/P. The valves operate in a split range configuration that is split pneumatically at the positioner. 0 to 100% output controls the A valve at 3 to 15psi and 0% to 100% controls the B valve at 9 to 15psi. Currently the A valve is broken and requires a total valve change. The B valve is not opening until the consistency is too far out of range to catch. The winder will have to be down for this valve change. We would like to temporarily add a low limit of 50% output to allow the B valve to act as the A valve.
